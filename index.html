Income Patterns and Uber Rides in NYC

Uber is supposed to be the future of transporation and even replacement for people who do not own cars. However, it seems that Uber is being used in higher-income locations in New York City.


New York City Uber Pickups

The most active and highest income locations in New York City appear to be in lower Manhattan.


Ride Statistics Per Day

Peak pickup time each day is generally around 4pm, so we can assume people are going home from work. But rather than serving the areas that are not as wealthy and do not have as many subway stops, there is generally a large concentration pickups in lower Manhattan and Brooklyn.
